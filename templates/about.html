The University of Iowa COVID-19 Monitoring System

#all pictures are from Uiowa webpage

Why we need this Monitoring System?

As we know, due to the impact of the epidemic, the University of Iowa has changed from face-to-face classes to virtual classes by the spring of 2020. The impact of the epidemic has profoundly affected the normal operation of the university. In the future of college life, we need a COVID-19 monitoring system, which can easily know the physical conditions of students and teachers, so as to ensure the safety of the people of the University of Iowa and the operation of the school.

How does the Monitoring System work?

As a student or professor, all need to report their healthy condions information everyday. Those information include your HawkID, First_name, Last_name, your Temperature in C everyday, FeelingGood or not, NeedHelp or not, TodayClass information, whether you contact with sick people, and the report date. Our employee will monitor those information closely. For people who feeling not good, the system can quickly find out, so that we can have a plan at the first time. Under such circumstances, the safety of students and teachers can be guaranteed.
